ECO4: TOWARDS NET ZERO EMISSIONS

ECO4 is the fourth phase of the Energy Company Obligation scheme, a government-funded program that aims to improve the energy efficiency of homes in the UK. The scheme provides funding for a range of energy-saving measures, such as home insulation, boiler upgrades, and renewable energy installations. Our mission is to create a clean future for all.

Mission Statement

The UK government has introduced the ECO4 scheme so homeowners, private tenants and landlords can apply for a government grant. ECO4 is committed to creating a sustainable future by reducing greenhouse gas emissions and being eco-friendly. Anybody living in a properties that were not eligible for the ECO4 scheme missed out on energy savings. ECO4 intends to reduce carbon emissions and empower communities to take charge of their own environment. The scheme has access to funding from the UK government and covers for all the costs. Another objective is to reduce fuel poverty by using renewable and clean energy like solar energy.

Eligibility

You must:

Be a home owner or private tenant in Wales or England

Have electric storage heaters

Have an Energy Performance Certificate rating of D, E or F

Have any of the following benefits:

  1. Child Tax Credits
  2. Universal Credit
  3. Housing Benefit
  4. Income Support
  5. Child Benefit and Working Tax Credit
